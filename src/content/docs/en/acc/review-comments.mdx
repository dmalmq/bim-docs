## Leaving a comment as a reviewer

The first flow I am showing is of the person in charge of reviewing the model. In order to add annotations inside of ACC we first need to open the Revit model.

<Steps>
1. Open the model inside of ACC
![step one](./img/acc_draw_step_1.png)

2. Click on the **Sheets and Views** icon from the toolbar.
   ![step one](./img/acc_draw_step_2.png)

3. Click on the '2D' tab
   ![step one](./img/acc_draw_step_3.png)

4. Select the drawing you would like to leave your comment on.
   ![step one](./img/acc_draw_step_4.png)

5. Set the **priority** (Critical / Major / Minor).
   ![step one](./img/acc_draw_step_5.png)
</Steps>

## Responding to Comments

When a comment is assigned to you it will appear in your ACC notification panel.

<Steps>
  1. Open the comment from your **notifications** or from the **Issues** tab. 2.
  Review the markup in context on the model. 3. Reply with your resolution or
  questions. 4. Once resolved, mark the comment as **Closed**.
</Steps>

## Review Workflow

For formal review cycles (e.g. before issuing for construction), we create a
**Review** in ACC rather than using ad-hoc comments. This groups all feedback
into a single trackable session with a deadline.

<Aside type="caution">
  Do not close a Review until the Project Lead has confirmed all issues are
  addressed.
</Aside>
