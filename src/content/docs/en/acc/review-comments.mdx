This guide walks through the review workflow for the person responsible for reviewing a model. To add annotations inside ACC, you first need to open the Revit model.

<Steps>
1. Open the **project** folder
   ![step 1](./img/acc_draw_step_1.png)

2. If the model you are looking for is not the main model, click on **Links**
   ![step 2](./img/acc_draw_step_2.png)

3. Click on the **Revit model** you want to review
   ![step 3](./img/acc_draw_step_3.png)

4. Click on **Sheets & Views**
   ![step 4](./img/acc_draw_step_4.png)

5. Click on the **2D** tab
   ![step 5](./img/acc_draw_step_5.png)

6. Choose the **2D Drawing** you want to review/comment on
   ![step 6](./img/acc_draw_step_6.png)

7. Select an **annotation tool** and mark the area you want to comment on
   ![step 7](./img/acc_draw_step_7.png)

8. Use the selected tool to **highlight** the area you want to comment on
   ![step 8](./img/acc_draw_step_8.png)

9. After highlighting the area, click on **Issues**
   ![step 9](./img/acc_draw_step_9.png)

10. Select the category of the issue, usually **General**
    ![step 10](./img/acc_draw_step_10.png)

11. Click inside the highlighted **area** you created earlier
    ![step 11](./img/acc_draw_step_11.png)

12. Add a **title** to your issue
    ![step 12](./img/acc_draw_step_12.png)

13. Click on the highlighted area to **assign** a person to the issue
    ![step 13](./img/acc_draw_step_13.png)

14. Select the person you would like to assign from the **drop down**
    ![step 14](./img/acc_draw_step_14.png)

15. Click on **Description**
    ![step 15](./img/acc_draw_step_15.png)

16. Write a **description** for the issue. Make sure it is easy to understand.
    ![step 16](./img/acc_draw_step_16.png)

17. Click on the **checkmark** button to add the description to the issue
    ![step 17](./img/acc_draw_step_17.png)

18. Click on the highlighted area to add a **due date**
    ![step 18](./img/acc_draw_step_18.png)

19. Choose a **date** from the calendar to set when you want the issue resolved by
    ![step 19](./img/acc_draw_step_19.png)

20. Click on the highlighted area to add a **start date**
    ![step 20](./img/acc_draw_step_20.png)

21. Choose a **date** from the calendar for when the assigned person should start working on the issue
    ![step 21](./img/acc_draw_step_21.png)

22. Close the issue window by clicking the **X** in the top right corner. Your issue has been saved.
    ![step 22](./img/acc_draw_step_22.png)
</Steps>
