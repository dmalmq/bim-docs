This guide walks through the review workflow for the person responsible for reviewing a model. To add annotations inside ACC, you first need to open the Revit model.

## Open the Model

<Steps>
1. Open the **project** folder
   ![step 1](/images/acc/acc_draw_step_1.png)

2. If the model you are looking for is not the main model, click on **Links**
   ![step 2](/images/acc/acc_draw_step_2.png)

3. Click on the **Revit model** you want to review
   ![step 3](/images/acc/acc_draw_step_3.png)
</Steps>

## Navigate to Drawing

<Steps>
1. Click on **Sheets & Views**
   ![step 4](/images/acc/acc_draw_step_4.png)

2. Click on the **2D** tab
   ![step 5](/images/acc/acc_draw_step_5.png)

3. Choose the **2D Drawing** you want to review/comment on
   ![step 6](/images/acc/acc_draw_step_6.png)
</Steps>

## Annotate the Drawing

<Steps>
1. Select an **annotation tool** and mark the area you want to comment on
   ![step 7](/images/acc/acc_draw_step_7.png)

2. Use the selected tool to **highlight** the area you want to comment on
   ![step 8](/images/acc/acc_draw_step_8.png)
</Steps>

## Create an Issue

<Steps>
1. After highlighting the area, click on **Issues**
   ![step 9](/images/acc/acc_draw_step_9.png)

2. Select the category of the issue, usually **General**
   ![step 10](/images/acc/acc_draw_step_10.png)

3. Click inside the highlighted **area** you created earlier
   ![step 11](/images/acc/acc_draw_step_11.png)

4. Add a **title** to your issue
   ![step 12](/images/acc/acc_draw_step_12.png)
</Steps>

## Fill in Issue Details

<Steps>
1. Click on the highlighted area to **assign** a person to the issue
   ![step 13](/images/acc/acc_draw_step_13.png)

2. Select the person you would like to assign from the **drop down**
   ![step 14](/images/acc/acc_draw_step_14.png)

3. Click on **Description**
   ![step 15](/images/acc/acc_draw_step_15.png)

4. Write a **description** for the issue. Make sure it is easy to understand.
   ![step 16](/images/acc/acc_draw_step_16.png)

5. Click on the **checkmark** button to add the description to the issue
   ![step 17](/images/acc/acc_draw_step_17.png)

6. Click on the highlighted area to add a **due date**
   ![step 18](/images/acc/acc_draw_step_18.png)

7. Choose a **date** from the calendar to set when you want the issue resolved by
   ![step 19](/images/acc/acc_draw_step_19.png)

8. Click on the highlighted area to add a **start date**
   ![step 20](/images/acc/acc_draw_step_20.png)

9. Choose a **date** from the calendar for when the assigned person should start working on the issue
   ![step 21](/images/acc/acc_draw_step_21.png)

10. Close the issue window by clicking the **X** in the top right corner. Your issue has been saved.
    ![step 22](/images/acc/acc_draw_step_22.png)
</Steps>
